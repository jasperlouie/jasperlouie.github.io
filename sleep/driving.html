Got Sleep?
Based on a study by Powell et al., after a person has been awake for 22 hours straight, their awareness is equal to, or worse than, that of a person whose blood alcohol level would cause them to illegally drive.
Tell me more!

The study was done using a psychomotor vigilence test which asks the user to press a button when it lights up. When a subject is more intoxicated or sleepy, they tend to miss a cue to press the button, either from inattention or dampened reflexes.

But there is more proof:

In another study by Varughese et al., there is a marked increase in accidents when people just lose one hour of sleep during daylight savings time. This causes a 15% increase in car accidents, including those that are fatal.
